Welcome to the Data Visualization Module

What is Data Visualization?

Data visualization is the graphical representation of information and data. By using visual elements like charts, graphs, and maps, data visualization tools provide an accessible way to see and understand trends, outliers, and patterns in data.

In this module, we will explore how to process and present data in a way that tells a compelling story. You will learn to create dynamic and interactive visualizations that help audiences grasp complex information quickly and efficiently.

Why is Data Visualization Important?

Data visualization plays a key role in making complex data more digestible. It helps:

  • Identify trends and patterns that may not be immediately obvious in raw data.
  • Make data-driven decisions by transforming numbers into visual representations.
  • Communicate findings effectively to stakeholders, enabling better understanding and action.

Module Goals
